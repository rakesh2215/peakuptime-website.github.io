About PeakUptime

Reliable connectivity for coworking spaces. Built with engineering discipline.

Our Mission

PeakUptime ensures that coworking spaces run on stable, predictable internet — free from sudden outages, mystery drops, and ISP surprises. We monitor ISPs, access points, CCTV/NVRs, and client activity, giving you a clear picture of your network at all times.

Why We Exist

For most teams, WiFi is the office’s lifeline. Still, diagnosing issues takes hours — and coworking admins aren't network engineers. PeakUptime removes this burden entirely:

  • Real-time monitoring of every ISP and access point
  • Automatic WhatsApp alerts when anything breaks
  • Detailed histories of uptime, load, users, and speeds
  • Zero hardware cost — we deploy our appliance for free

Built With Engineering Discipline

PeakUptime is built by engineers who’ve spent years working on networking, embedded systems, and real-time monitoring software. Our focus is simple: deliver high uptime, low noise, and clear insights. No buzzwords. No fluff. Just reliability.

Who Uses PeakUptime?

Today, PeakUptime is deployed across coworking spaces in Bangalore — tracking internet health, sending automated alerts, and helping admins stay ahead of problems instead of reacting to them.
